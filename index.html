About Us

LinkFactor utilizes a system that applies a very standards compliant implementation of the FHIR Questionnaires to track and record data about patients.

Further, LinkFactor provides real time virtual assistant chatbot that asks patients about their symptoms and provides advice for the symptoms. There is also virtual meeting room service with video calling for connecting the patients with doctors for consultation. LinkFactor provides patients questions that ask for their consent to share their data and willingness to recontact in all of our services.
